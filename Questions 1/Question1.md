## Q4: Check for Leap Year

**Question:** Write a C++ program to check whether a given year is a leap year or not.  
**Concept:** Compound conditional statements, modulo operator  
**Input:** A year (integer)  
**Output:** Display whether the year is a leap year or not  
**Logic:**

- A year is a leap year if: (divisible by 4 AND NOT divisible by 100) OR (divisible by 400)

## Q5: Calculate Wall Area of a Room

**Question:** Write a C++ program to calculate the total wall area of a room given its length, width, and height.  
**Concept:** Mathematical calculations with floating-point numbers  
**Input:** Length, width, and height of the room (floats)  
**Output:** Total wall area  
**Formula:** Wall Area = 2(length × height) + 2(width × height)
